### Navigation Plan

Starting in the GARAGES, the goal is to reach the CELLIER. The most efficient path involves the following sequence of actions:

```json
{
  "navigation_plan": [
    "ApproachDoor(D1)",
    "OpenDoor(D1)",
    "GoThrough(D1)",
    "ApproachDoor(D3)",
    "OpenDoor(D3)",
    "GoThrough(D3)",
    "GoTo(CELLIER)"
  ]
}
```

### Explanation:
1. **ApproachDoor(D1)**: Move in front of door D1.
2. **OpenDoor(D1)**: Open door D1.
3. **GoThrough(D1)**: Move through door D1 to the HALL.
4. **ApproachDoor(D3)**: Move in front of door D3.
5. **OpenDoor(D3)**: Open door D3.
6. **GoThrough(D3)**: Move through door D3 to the CELLIER.
7. **GoTo(CELLIER)**: Move to the CELLIER.

This plan ensures that you navigate from the GARAGES to the CELLIER efficiently, using only the necessary doors.
