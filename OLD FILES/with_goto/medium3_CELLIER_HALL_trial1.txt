```json
[
    {"action": "ApproachDoor", "door": "D3"},
    {"action": "OpenDoor", "door": "D3"},
    {"action": "GoThrough", "door": "D3"},
    {"action": "GoTo", "location": "HALL"}

```

This plan ensures that you move from the CELLIER to the HALL by approaching, opening, and going through the necessary door (D3) and then moving to the HALL.
