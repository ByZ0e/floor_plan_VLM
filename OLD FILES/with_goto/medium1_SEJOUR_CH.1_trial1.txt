### Navigation Plan

To navigate from SEJOUR to CH.1, the following sequence of actions is required:

```json
{
  "actions": [
    "ApproachDoor(D4)",
    "OpenDoor(D4)",
    "GoThrough(D4)",
    "ApproachDoor(D5)",
    "OpenDoor(D5)",
    "GoThrough(D5)",
    "GoTo(CH.1)"
  ]
}
```

### Explanation

1. **ApproachDoor(D4)**: Move in front of door D4, which connects SEJOUR to DEGT.
2. **OpenDoor(D4)**: Open door D4.
3. **GoThrough(D4)**: Move through the open door D4 to enter DEGT.
4. **ApproachDoor(D5)**: Move in front of door D5, which connects DEGT to CH.1.
5. **OpenDoor(D5)**: Open door D5.
6. **GoThrough(D5)**: Move through the open door D5 to enter CH.1.
7. **GoTo(CH.1)**: Move to the specified location within CH.1.
