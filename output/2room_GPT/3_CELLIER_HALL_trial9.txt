### Navigation Plan

To navigate from CELLIER to HALL, the following steps should be taken:

1. **ApproachDoor(11)**: Move in front of door D11.
2. **OpenDoor(11)**: Open door D11.
3. **GoThrough(11)**: Move through door D11 to the HALL.
