### Navigation Plan

To navigate from the CELLIER to the HALL, the following sequence of actions is required:

```json
[
    {"action": "ApproachDoor", "door": "D3"},
    {"action": "OpenDoor", "door": "D3"},
    {"action": "GoThrough", "door": "D3"},
    {"action": "ApproachDoor", "door": "D4"},
    {"action": "OpenDoor", "door": "D4"},
    {"action": "GoThrough", "door": "D4"},
    {"action": "GoTo", "location": "HALL"}
]
```

### Explanation

1. **ApproachDoor(D3)**: Move in front of door D3.
2. **OpenDoor(D3)**: Open door D3.
3. **GoThrough(D3)**: Move through door D3 to the WC.
4. **ApproachDoor(D4)**: Move in front of door D4.
5. **OpenDoor(D4)**: Open door D4.
6. **GoThrough(D4)**: Move through door D4 to the HALL.
7. **GoTo(HALL)**: Move to the HALL location.

This sequence ensures that you navigate efficiently from the CELLIER to the HALL, passing through the necessary doors without any unnecessary steps.
