### Navigation Plan:

To navigate from the HALL to the CUISINE, you need to use the following doors:

1. **ApproachDoor(D5):** Move in front of door D5.
2. **OpenDoor(D5):** Open door D5.
3. **GoThrough(D5):** Move through open door D5 to the CUISINE.
