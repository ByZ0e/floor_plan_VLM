### Room and Door Connections

- **D1**: Connects HALL and BATHROOM
- **D2**: Connects CH.1 and HALL
- **D3**: Connects CH.2 and HALL
- **D4**: Connects HALL and GARAGE
- **D5**: Connects HALL and CUISINE

### Navigation Plan

1. **ApproachDoor(2)**: Move in front of door D2.
2. **OpenDoor(2)**: Open door D2.
3. **GoThrough(2)**: Move through door D2 to HALL.
4. **ApproachDoor(4)**: Move in front of door D4.
5. **OpenDoor(4)**: Open door D4.
6. **GoThrough(4)**: Move through door D4 to GARAGE.
